About Paul

My name is Christopher Paul Boulanger but my friends call me Paul. Though I initially worked in the sporting goods industry (cycling and skiing), I always maintained a side business providing repair services to family, friends, and subsequently my new clients through numerous referrals.

For the last 25 years I worked for a clothing retailer providing National Repair Services to over 200 stores across Canada. I personally performed all the local work and oversaw contractors from coast to coast to ensure all store repairs were done not only in a timely manner but also cost effectively.

An avid skier, 6 years ago I decided to make Blue Mountains my home.

After spending most of my career working for others, I now exclusively offer my wide range of repair services to the residents and business owners of the Georgian Triangle.

C Paul B for all your Residential and Commercial Repairs!
